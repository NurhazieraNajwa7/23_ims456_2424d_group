Strengths:

The company offers high-quality hijabs in various designs, fabrics, and colors, catering to a diverse customer base. The hijab company has built a strong brand image and has gained a loyal customer following due to its commitment to quality and style. The company has a robust online presence, with an e-commerce website and active social media channels, enabling it to reach a broader audience and expand its customer base. Weaknesses:

The company's distribution network is primarily focused on the domestic market, and it faces challenges in expanding its presence globally. Offering customization options for hijabs can attract customers looking for unique and personalized products. Introducing innovative hijab designs or accessories can differentiate the company from competitors and appeal to fashion-forward consumers.
